## Registration Management

### Creating Registration Forms

Design custom registration experiences that align with your sustainability goals:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Form Builder">
    Use our drag-and-drop form builder to create:

    **Standard Fields:**
    - Name, email, organization, job title
    - Dietary preferences (with plant-based option highlighting)
    - T-shirt size (with sustainable merchandise option)
    - Accessibility requirements

    **Sustainability Fields:**
    - Transportation method (for carbon calculation)
    - Carbon offset opt-in with pricing
    - Digital vs. printed materials preference
    - Participation in green initiatives (tree planting, beach cleanup, etc.)
    - Sustainability interests and priorities

    **Custom Fields:**
    - Add unlimited custom questions
    - Support for text, multiple choice, checkboxes, dropdowns
    - Conditional logic based on previous answers
    - File uploads for required documentation
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pricing & Tickets">
    Configure ticket types and pricing:

    - **Early Bird Tickets** - Reward advance planning
    - **Green Discount** - Offer reduced rates for attendees who commit to sustainable choices (public transit, plant-based meals, carbon offset)
    - **Tiered Pricing** - General admission, VIP, student, group rates
    - **Add-Ons** - Workshops, meals, merchandise, carbon offsets
    - **Promo Codes** - Create discount codes for partners and sponsors

    <Note>
      Consider offering a 10-15% "Green Attendee" discount for those who choose all sustainable options. This incentivizes eco-friendly behavior and typically increases participation rates.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Payment Processing">
    Secure payment collection with sustainability tracking:

    - Accept credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers
    - Automatic carbon offset calculation and collection
    - Donation matching for environmental causes
    - Transparent fee breakdown showing sustainability investments
    - Invoice generation for corporate attendees
    - Refund management with policy enforcement
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Confirmation & Communication">
    Automated post-registration engagement:

    - Instant confirmation emails with QR code tickets
    - Sustainability guidelines and green travel tips
    - Calendar invitations (.ics files)
    - Pre-event reminders with actionable eco-tips
    - Personalized recommendations based on registration data
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Check-In System

### Digital Check-In Options

EcoEvents offers multiple contactless, paperless check-in methods:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="QR Code Scanning" icon="qrcode">
    Fastest option for high-volume events
    - Attendees receive unique QR codes via email
    - Staff scan codes using mobile app or tablet
    - Instant validation and badge printing
    - Works offline with background sync
  </Card>
  <Card title="Self-Service Kiosks" icon="tablet-screen">
    Reduce staffing requirements
    - Attendees check themselves in at kiosks
    - QR code or email lookup
    - Self-print digital badges
    - Collect additional on-site information
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile App Check-In" icon="mobile">
    Most eco-friendly option
    - Attendees use smartphone as badge
    - NFC or Bluetooth beacon validation
    - Zero printed materials
    - Real-time location tracking (opt-in)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Facial Recognition" icon="face-smile">
    Cutting-edge convenience
    - Pre-upload photos during registration
    - Walk-through check-in with no device needed
    - GDPR/privacy compliant
    - Optional feature for willing attendees
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Badge Management

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Digital Badges">
    The most sustainable option - no physical materials:

    - Display on attendee smartphone via mobile app or web
    - Includes QR code for session access and networking
    - Real-time updates (schedule changes, announcements)
    - Interactive features (agenda, maps, vendor info)
    - Export to Apple Wallet or Google Pay

    <Note>
      Digital badges reduce waste by 100% and save an average of $3-5 per attendee in printing costs.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Eco-Friendly Physical Badges">
    For events requiring physical badges:

    - **Recycled Materials** - Print on 100% post-consumer recycled cardstock
    - **Reusable Holders** - Bamboo or recycled plastic lanyards with collection bins
    - **Seed Paper Badges** - Plantable badges embedded with wildflower seeds
    - **Minimal Design** - Black and white printing to reduce ink usage
    - **Digital Hybrid** - Small physical badge with QR code linking to digital info

    Design templates ensure optimal use of badge stock with minimal waste.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Badge Customization">
    Personalize badges for different attendee types:

    - Color coding by ticket type or access level
    - Custom fields (name, organization, pronouns, social handle)
    - Session access indicators
    - Sponsor branding zones
    - Sustainability badges ("Green Attendee", "Carbon Neutral")
    - Accessibility indicators (optional)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Attendee Communications

### Email Campaigns

Keep attendees informed with targeted, eco-conscious messaging:

<Accordion title="Pre-Event Sequence">
  Automated email series leading up to the event:

  **Registration Confirmation** (Immediate)
  - Thank you message with ticket details
  - QR code for check-in
  - Sustainability overview and your event's green goals

  **Preparation Guide** (2-3 weeks before)
  - Event logistics and what to expect
  - Green travel options and directions
  - Packing list emphasizing reusable items (water bottle, utensils)
  - Carbon offset reminder for those who opted out

  **Schedule Released** (1 week before)
  - Full agenda with session descriptions
  - Sustainability highlights and eco-workshops
  - Mobile app download links
  - Last-minute travel tips

  **Final Reminders** (1-2 days before)
  - Check-in process and timing
  - Weather forecast and recommendations
  - Parking vs. public transit options
  - What to bring / what not to bring

  **Day-Of** (Morning of event)
  - Good morning message and excitement builder
  - Real-time updates (traffic, parking, delays)
  - First session reminders
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="During-Event Messaging">
  Keep attendees engaged throughout:

  - **Session Reminders** - Notifications 10 minutes before sessions
  - **Schedule Changes** - Instant alerts for room changes or cancellations
  - **Announcements** - Important updates and emergency information
  - **Sustainability Milestones** - Real-time impact achievements ("We've diverted 500 lbs from landfills!")
  - **Interactive Polls** - Engagement questions and feedback collection
  - **Networking Prompts** - Connect attendees with similar interests
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Post-Event Follow-Up">
  Maintain relationships and gather insights:

  **Immediate** (Within 24 hours)
  - Thank you message
  - Survey link for feedback
  - Session recordings and slides (if available)
  - Sustainability impact summary

  **Follow-Up** (1 week after)
  - Detailed impact report with infographics
  - Carbon footprint calculation for their participation
  - Certificate of attendance (digital)
  - Future event announcements
  - Community continuation (Slack/Discord invites)

  **Long-Term** (Monthly/quarterly)
  - Newsletter with sustainability news
  - Early access to future event registration
  - Case studies and success stories
</Accordion>

### SMS Notifications

For time-sensitive updates (requires opt-in):

- Event day reminders and check-in times
- Urgent schedule changes or emergencies
- Queue management ("Your session starts in 10 mins")
- Parking and traffic alerts
- Sustainability challenges and gamification

<Warning>
  Keep SMS messages brief and valuable. Over-messaging can lead to opt-outs. Reserve SMS for truly important updates.
</Warning>

### Data Export & Integration

Get your data where you need it:

```javascript

const attendees = await ecoEvents.attendees.list({
  eventId: 'evt_12345',
  filters: {
    ticketType: 'general-admission',
    checkedIn: true,
    carbonOffsetOptIn: true

  fields: ['name', 'email', 'organization', 'sustainabilityChoices']
```

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Optimize for Mobile">
  Most attendees will interact via smartphones:
  - Keep registration forms concise (8-10 fields maximum)
  - Use mobile-responsive design
  - Prioritize digital badges over printed
  - Test check-in flow on multiple devices
  - Ensure QR codes are easily accessible
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Educate and Incentivize">
  Encourage sustainable choices:
  - Clearly explain environmental impact of choices
  - Offer discounts for green options (5-15% effective)
  - Gamify sustainability (points, badges, leaderboards)
  - Share real-time progress toward sustainability goals
  - Recognize green champions publicly
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Prepare for On-Site Issues">
  Have contingency plans:
  - Offline check-in capability (critical!)
  - Backup devices and chargers
  - Paper backup list (as absolute last resort)
  - Clear staff training and troubleshooting guides
  - Dedicated support person for attendee issues
</Accordion>
